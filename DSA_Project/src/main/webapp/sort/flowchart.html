📚 What are Sorting Flowcharts?

Visual representations of sorting algorithms showing the step-by-step logic of comparison, swapping, and termination conditions.

🔑 Key Algorithm Categories:

  • 💭 Comparison-based: Bubble, Selection, Insertion, Quick, Merge
  • 📊 Distribution-based: Counting, Radix, Bucket
  • 🔄 Hybrid: Shell, Timsort, Heap
  • 🎪 Fun/Obscure: Bogo, Pancake, Stooge

Each flowchart reveals unique optimization strategies and trade-offs in time/space complexity.
